What is Telework

Understanding the answer to "What is telework?" can help you clarify the difference between telework vs. remote work. With telework, employees typically work near the physical office. For example, you may live in the same city as your office but complete your work-related tasks from home or at another nearby location, such as co-working space. Telework can also include spending part of your working hours at a different place than the physical office.

What is Technology

They are widely used in medicine, science, industry, communication, transportation, and daily life.
